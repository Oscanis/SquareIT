SquareIT!

A game about squares and matching numbers

Rules:

  • Match the numbers on the adjacent sides of the squares.
  • You can achieve this by rotating a square to the right.
  • You have limited number of steps, so think ahead!
  • The final score is the sum of the paired numbers.
  • Toplist works with default table size only: 4x3, with 10 steps
